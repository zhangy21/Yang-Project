In fact, most of the edible salt in China comes from mineral salt, which is a mineral containing salts formed naturally underground or on the surface of the earth. These minerals are mined, refined and processed to form the salt we use in our daily lives. China is rich in mineral salt resources and can produce more than 1,000 tons per year, which is enough to ensure the national demand for salt. Compared with sea salt, mineral salt is safer and more stable, and is less susceptible to the influence of the external environment. In the production process of edible salt will also go through processing, purification and other procedures to ensure safety.
